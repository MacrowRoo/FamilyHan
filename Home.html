FamilyHan

FamilyHan is a company founded by  five members. Our goal in FamilyHan is to primarily help farmers and crops hobbyists alike in the local communities here in the Philippines to sell their product directly to the people, and also to help the people to fresh and secured sources of food grown and cultivated by their fellow citizens.
